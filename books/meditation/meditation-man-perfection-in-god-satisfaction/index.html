Meditation: Man-Perfection in God-Satisfaction

Highly recommended!

Learn to meditate or deepen your existing practice with the most complete and easy to understand book on meditation available.

Presented with the simplicity and clarity that are the hallmarks of Sri Chinmoy’s writing, this book will take you from the beginning stages of concentration and meditation through the advanced practice of contemplation.

Topics include:

  • Meditation techniques that anyone can learn
  • Developing your power of concentration
  • The powerful relationship between prayer and meditation
  • How to find—and keep—lasting peace of mind
  • How to break through negative emotions like anger and fear
  • Recognizing and interpreting the “Voice of Silence” within you
  • Awakening the heart centre to discover the power of your soul
  • How to unfold your deepest spiritual self

Plus a special section in which Sri Chinmoy answers 150 questions on a wide range of experiences often encountered in meditation. This is an enduring favourite, now in its 10th printing.

Published by Aum Publications, NY – 304 pages

Reviews

This is a great book! If you read this book with an open mind and open heart you will “experience” meditation while reading. Sri Chinmoy expresses the beauty of the inner life with his poetic use of language (like all true masters), yet with a purity, simplicity and clarity that reaches and teaches the heart rather than the mind. There is no doubt in reading this book that Sri Chinmoy is writing from his direct and first-hand experiences and is clearly a leading authority on this subject.

I found this such a deeply moving book! The author explains to us all about different meditation practices right from the basics to the more advanced levels. This is for anyone interested in meditation as a practice, as well as someone with just a casual interest. A deeply moving book, I recommend it to everyone!

For several years i have used this as the recommended textbook for a meditation course taught at the University of Auckland, New Zealand. Many students buy the book and report that they find the exercises and guidance very helpful. One of the great things about the book is that both beginners and advanced meditators can return to it and each time find something new as they progress. An invaluable purchase for anyone following or wishing to start a meditation practice.
